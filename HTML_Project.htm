Save the Bees!

Bees are a keystone species vital to human life and environmental biodiversity. They pollinate a wide range of crops, including those used for animal feed, which ultimately helps feed us. Their honey and other byproducts have long been used in medicine, and their pollination reduces agricultural costs by improving yields naturally. With many specialized species, bees support plant diversity and healthy ecosystems. However, their numbers are declining due to climate change, pesticides, invasive species, habitat loss, and disease, threatening global food production and agricultural goals.
